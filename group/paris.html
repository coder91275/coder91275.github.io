Travel to Paris, France

Paris, France

France is known as the City of Love.

The things to see and do in France and the surrounding areas are endless. Below are a few of my favorite things.

Places To Go:

Palace of Versaille

The Palace of Versailles opulent, full o f priceless art. However, the most stunning artistry in the Palace, in my opinion, are the ceilings. The artwork is breathtaking and well worth a visit. For additional information click here.

Porte de Clignancourt – Flea Market

The most famous flea market in Paris is the one at Porte de Clignancourt, officially called Les Puces de Saint-Ouen, but known to everyone as Les Puces (The Fleas). See here for additional information, which includes days and hours of operations.

Bizz’Art

This is a great restaurant/club for lovers of soul and Latin dance music. It’s a restaurant, bar, concerts hall, all in one. Click (here ) to learn more.
